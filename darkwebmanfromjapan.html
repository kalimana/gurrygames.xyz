ABOUT

Dark Web Man From Japan places you in front of a computer screen, forcing you to explore the darkest corners of the deep web while surviving real-world threats that escalate in real time.

As you dig deeper, paranoia sets in. Power outages, home invasions, and unseen enemies ensure there is never a safe moment.
