About the Game

Stalker Man From Japan explores the blurred line between creator and viewer. As a small streamer living alone, routine begins to crack as messages, deliveries, and encounters grow increasingly personal — and invasive.

The horror unfolds slowly through everyday actions, environmental storytelling, and unsettling interactions that feel disturbingly real.

GenrePsychological Horror

What to Expect

Stalker Man From Japan avoids traditional jump-scare horror in favor of slow-burn unease. Mundane tasks slowly become threatening as boundaries are crossed and safety feels less certain.

  • Realistic modern environments
  • Subtle, escalating tension
  • Storytelling through routine and disruption
  • Moments designed to feel uncomfortably personal

Recommended: Play with headphones, lights off, alone.
